# Impact Model — AI Money Mentor

## Problem

**95% of Indians have no financial plan.** The remaining 5% pay ₹25,000–₹50,000/year for a human financial advisor.

AI Money Mentor democratizes personalized financial advice at zero cost.

---

## Quantified User Impact

### 1. Tax Optimization — ₹38,000 average annual saving

| Scenario | Old Regime Tax | New Regime Tax | Saving |
|----------|---------------|----------------|--------|
| ₹10L salary, no deductions | ₹1,12,500 | ₹75,400 | ₹37,100 |
| ₹18L salary, partial deductions | ₹3,04,200 | ₹2,35,000 | ₹69,200 |
| ₹8L salary, optimal old regime | ₹52,500 | ₹62,500 | ₹10,000 (old better) |

**Missed deduction recovery (₹18L salary example):**
- 80C shortfall ₹1L → saves ₹30,000 (at 30% bracket)
- 80CCD(1B) NPS ₹50K → saves ₹15,000
- 80D ₹10K → saves ₹3,000
- **Total recovered: ₹48,000/year**

**Average across salary ranges ₹6L–₹25L: ₹38,000/year**

Without a plan: investors under-invest by ~30% (common finding in AMFI data).

With AI Money Mentor's FIRE planner:
